Explore Our Diverse Range of Attractions

From the moment you step foot in Go Land, you'll be immersed in a world of fun and excitement. Our attractions offer something for everyone, whether you're a thrill-seeker, a nature lover, or a fan of classic amusement park rides. Come and see why Go Land is the ultimate destination for unforgettable adventures.

Experience the Rush

Speed Thrills

Hold on tight as you zoom through twists and turns on our Speed Thrills roller coaster. Feel the wind in your hair and the adrenaline pumping as you race through exhilarating loops and gravity-defying drops. A heart-pounding adventure awaits!

Make a Splash

Splash Island

Cool off and get ready for thrills at Splash Island! Brave the rapids as you navigate through swirling waters and cascading waterfalls. Whether you're seeking excitement or relaxation, this water ride promises fun for the whole family!

Reach for the Sky

Sky High Vista

Take in breathtaking views from the top of Sky High Vista, our iconic Ferris wheel. Soar high above the park as you enjoy panoramic vistas of the surrounding landscape. A leisurely ride with unforgettable sights awaits!

Enter the Unknown

Mystic Manor

Embark on a journey into the mysterious world of Mystic Manor. Encounter enchanted creatures and mystical landscapes as you navigate through this immersive dark ride. Prepare to be enchanted by the magic that awaits!

Step into Fantasy

Whimsical Wonder

Let your imagination soar on Whimsical Wonder, our enchanting carousel ride. Choose your favorite steed and enjoy a whimsical journey through a world of fantasy and fun. A magical experience for riders of all ages!

Conquer Your Fears

Vertigo Plunge

Brace yourself for an adrenaline-pumping experience on Vertigo Plunge, our towering drop tower ride. Feel the anticipation build as you ascend to dizzying heights before plummeting back to earth in a thrilling free-fall.
